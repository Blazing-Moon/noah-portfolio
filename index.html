Noah Giesler makes films, photographs,

I'm a cinematographer, gaffer, and composer based in the Midwest. My work lives in the quiet spaces — in the shape of light on a face, the texture of a held frame, the score that arrives one beat later than you expect. I'm drawn to projects where sound, image, and restraint do more work than spectacle.
