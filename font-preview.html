Option A

Plus Jakarta Sans 800 (Extra Bold) for headings · Plus Jakarta Sans 450 for body
One unified type family — headings are set at weight 800 vs. 450 for body. Clean, modern, cohesive. Works well if you want the type system to feel invisible/efficient rather than expressive.

Option B

DM Sans 700 (Bold) for headings · Plus Jakarta Sans 450 for body
DM Sans is a clean geometric sans from Colophon foundry. Very similar mood to Plus Jakarta Sans but with slightly softer, rounder letter forms — the two fonts complement each other well. Clear heading/body distinction without any "arty" feel.

Option C

Space Grotesk 700 (Bold) for headings · Plus Jakarta Sans 450 for body
Space Grotesk has clean geometric bones but with distinctive diagonal cuts on certain letters (e, a, s) that give it a bit of character without feeling decorative or "arty". Works well for film/creative portfolios — professional but not generic. The most visually differentiated of the three options.
